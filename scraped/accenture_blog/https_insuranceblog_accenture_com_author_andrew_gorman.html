Andrew Gorman

Andrew Gorman is a Senior Manager in Accenture’s Insurance management consulting business in Ireland, with responsibility for key Insurance client engagements in the Irish marketplace. Since joining Accenture in 2004, Andrew has spent much of his career delivering strategic change programmes for P&C clients, helping them transform to efficiently meet the ever-increasing expectations of customers. Andrew brings a wealth of experience, having spent time supporting clients often at the CxO level, in areas such as Operating Model Transformation, Core Systems Transformation and Strategic Cost Reduction. Andrew received a Degree in Business and Sociology from Trinity College Dublin and is based in Accenture’s Dublin office.
