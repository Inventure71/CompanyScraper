Industry Challenges

With surge in demand and seasonal shifts, the Third Party Logistics (3PL) industry requires a lot of flexibility and scalability. Some of the most faced challenges are:

1

Short-term contracts leading to redundancy of existing infrastructure

2

Changing market demands driving the needs for flexible automation solutions

3

Inadequate space utilisation for multi-customer site

4

Labour shortage to manage operations

5

Omnichannel fulfilment and complex returns management

Tailored automation solutions to fulfil your customers requirements

  • Storage

    Automated Storage & Retrieval System enables better space utilisation by providing dense storage and allows handling of large volume and variety of SKUs. Our Pallet and Crate ASRS solutions help 3PL customers to meet different operational requirements.

    Multi-Pro for carrying a pallet load of up to 1500 kg

    Cruiser for maximising storage density & operational productivity

    Crane-based ASRS for high accurate inventory management

  • Picking

    Our extensive solutions help players scale their warehousing operations with less labour.We support rainbow pallet formation, case picking with Pick-To-Light, Pick-By-Voice and our ergonomically designed goods-to-person picking tables.

    Veloce for rainbow pallet picking

    Rapdio for quick and accurate item picking

    Quadron for carrying a payload of up to 50 kg

  • Software

    Our versatile Warehouse Control System & Execution System manages material flow efficiently. It handles Multi-SKUs blending shared storage and automation effectively.

    WMS helps accurate inventory management

    WCS coordinates between different automation systems

    FMS helps to control a fleet of mobile robots
