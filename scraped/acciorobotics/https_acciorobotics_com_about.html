Our Journey

In 2019, a group of young college students, fueled by their passion for robotics, dared to challenge the norms of the supply chain industry, a sector typically dominated by seasoned professionals. Despite the industry's rigid conventions and the daunting task of gaining entry as newcomers, this ambitious team persevered. They confronted not only the technical challenges of innovation but also the formidable task of carving a space for themselves in an industry often resistant to change. Their journey stands as a vivid illustration of how adaptability and perseverance can bridge the gap between innovation and the wisdom of traditional practices, creating a new path forward.
