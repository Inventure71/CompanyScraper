Why AI Refinery matters

The ramp in demand for gen AI has been steep. Organizations initially focused on the art of the possible — innovation labs and proofs-of-concept — with mixed results. But now, to solution and activate at scale and show value, there are compound challenges. Enterprise leaders are faced with managing complexity, pace of change, cost, and risk across the domains of data, knowledge management, infrastructure, ecosystem, applications and agents, reputation, regulatory compliance, and security.

Accenture AI Refinery™ is a suite of asset-powered, expert-led solutions built on NVIDIA AI Foundry, NVIDIA AI Enterprise software (including NVIDIA NeMo, NVIDIA NIM microservices and NVIDIA AI Blueprints) and NVIDIA Omniverse, that drive value from investments in AI and generative AI.

AI Refinery™ Agent as a Service

Designed and developed by the Accenture Center for Advanced AI

AI RefineryTM Agent as a Service is a turn-key, agentic platform solution for faster agent development. It leverages the AI Refinery Distiller Framework, which establishes standards for LLM-based, multi-agent solutions. The Distiller Framework features advanced methodologies, including chain-of-thought reasoning, query transformation for agentic RAG, dynamic task decomposition and management and multi-agent memory synchronization. Through its advanced communication protocol, AI Refinery Agent as a Service seamlessly integrates with any existing library of agents for streamlined operations, scalability, and accelerated innovation.

How to get started

To help companies adopt enterprise AI faster, Accenture has developed AI Refinery™ for Industry, with industry agent solutions. These solutions, built on AI Refinery’s Agent as a Service and leveraging NVIDIA AI Enterprise, provide a set of pre-configured components, best practices, and foundational elements for an array of industries that help jumpstart agentic functionality.

The B2B Marketing agent solution transforms how marketing teams develop and manage complex campaigns by automating workflows and integrating data insights. Pre-built agent configurations reduce manual effort, speed deploy and enhance campaign quality for better ROI and faster growth.

Increased Speed

Optimize pricing and promotions to boost revenues in the consumer goods industry

The Revenue Growth Management solution unifies multiple agents to streamline pricing, promotions, and customer targeting. It leverages sequential optimization, real-time insights and “what-if” scenario models to assess the impact of decisions on key KPIs—yielding better, faster execution and accelerated growth.

Benefits

Empower field sales representatives in the consumer goods industry with real-time insights

The Intelligent Sales Coach agent solution empowers field sales reps in the consumer goods industry with tailored coaching and performance-enhancing tools, including retailer-specific sales pitches and daily tips to refine sales strategies. The solution integrates with existing systems like CRM, SFA and DMS to deliver real-time insights that improve sales outcomes and retailer engagement.
