Reinvented with Accenture
Vodafone Best Buy BBVA Other stories
Vodafone Best Buy BBVA Other stories

Welcome to Reinvented with Accenture, an exclusive interview series where Accenture CEO Julie Sweet engages with some of the worlds most influential business leaders to explore what it takes to thrive today, while reinventing for tomorrow.

Through candid conversations and thought-provoking questions, Julie uncovers the strategies, challenges, and breakthroughs that have shaped these reinvention journeys. From redefining industry standards to championing innovative solutions, each video is packed with wisdom and actionable advice for driving meaningful change across your organization.

01. Vodafone

View transcript

Julie Sweet and Margherita Della Valle discuss Vodafones reinvention, focusing on how the company is partnering with Accenture to commercialize its market leading shared services operations for industry customers in order to accelerate growth, enhance customer service, drive significant operational efficiencies and create new career opportunities for its people.

Watch the full interview

02. Best Buy

View transcript

Julie Sweet sits down with Corie Barry, Best Buy's dynamic CEO, to discuss Best Buys remarkable reinvention, from the demise of VCRs and compact discs to the surge in online shopping and implementing generative AI to enhance the customer experience. In an ever-evolving industry, Best Buy continues to transform its retail floors for new realms of technology by managing change and staying ahead in a rapidly shifting landscape.

Watch the full interview

03. BBVA

View transcript

BBVA has achieved resounding success reinventing itself for banking in the modern world. However, bringing about change in a bank with so much history hasn't been easy. Julie sits down with Carlos Torres Vila, BBVAs visionary chair, to discussthe companys reinvention, the 117% growth in new customers he has overseen, and how mobile is shaping a new performance frontier for the industry.

Watch the full interview
