What is myConcerto?

myConcerto brings together the best thought leadership, innovation and industry expertise from across Accenture, into a single, proprietary platform that simplifies and accelerates technology-led business transformation. Developed with people in mind—myConcerto expands the potential of your workforce with user-friendly tools designed to unlock value, faster.

Powered by our assets, processes, data and best practices across every industry and enterprise function. myConcerto allows you to benefit beyond your own industry, along with our deep expertise and capabilities of our intelligent platform partners SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, Workday, Microsoft and Adobe.

Accenture and myConcerto can meet you where you are on your journey—from program vision to mid-transformation and through to continuous innovation. myConcerto provides pre-defined templates and solutions for the program lifecycle with a focus on ROI to help you seek your desired outcomes and can ultimately help you in defining the right path for your business.

With myConcerto, you have insight into what’s working and what’s not–so you can take action and get back on track quickly.

Why use myConcerto?

myConcerto flexes to the unique needs and complexities of your business. With value realization and continuous innovation at its core—myConcerto helps take you from business case to business excellence—unlocking value faster.

myConcerto can help to reduce risk, improve ROI and increase speed to value by:

  • Providing insight into what’s working and what’s not through value tracking and analytics.
  • Pushing the boundaries of what technology can enable and expand the potential of your workforce to work smarter, not harder.
  • Establishing a culture of continuous innovation through the power of Accenture.

Orchestrate a tailored path by embracing change with myConcerto—The Intelligent Transformation Platform.
