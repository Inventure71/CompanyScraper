2016 Efma-Accenture Innovation in Insurance Awards Voting is Open


PARIS and NEW YORK; May 11, 2016 – Efma members have until 12 May to vote for the first-ever Efma-Accenture Innovation in Insurance Awards winners.

The Efma-Accenture Innovation in Insurance (I3) programme is a joint initiative that aims to identify the most innovative distribution and marketing projects by insurance companies across five continents.

The backbone of the programme is the I3 platform, which aims to highlight the insurance sector’s new challenges and showcase best practices worldwide. What makes the I3 programme unique is that this isn’t a commercial endeavour – it encourages recognition from industry peers.

“We’re creating a place where insurance companies and banks’ insurance subsidiaries will be able to showcase their innovations and get to grips with the latest ideas, technologies and strategies adopted by their peers,” explains Efma CEO Vincent Bastid.

Edwin VanderOuderaa, managing director, Accenture Digital, Financial Services, said, “Accenture is delighted to expand our relationship from working with Efma on the Banking Innovation Awards to include this joint initiative to foster innovation in the insurance industry, which is facing disruption at an unprecedented rate. We look forward to seeing how innovative insurers propose to address changing customer behaviors, new technologies and access to big data to help redefine their value proposition.”

Efma members can visit the awards website to learn more about the innovations and cast their vote. The best-in-class solutions will be chosen based on votes from both members and the Innovation Awards Committee, which comprises Efma board members and insurance executives from all over the world.

The winners will be revealed during the Innovation in Insurance Awards ceremony at the Insurance Summit (www.efma.com/insurance16), which will take place in Milan, Italy on 9 June. All Insurance Summit participants and speakers will be invited to the exclusive ceremony. Participants will learn about the industry’s challenges and hear from many respected figures in a relaxed atmosphere, becoming part of a community of top insurers who are specialists in distribution channels, transformation, business development, innovation, strategy and more.

About Efma
A global non-profit organisation, established in 1971 by banks and insurance companies, Efma facilitates networking between decision-makers. It provides quality insights to help banks and insurance companies make the right decisions to foster innovation and drive their transformation. Over 3,300 brands in 130 countries are Efma members.
Headquarters in Paris. Offices in London, Brussels, Barcelona, Stockholm, Bratislava, Dubai, Mumbai and Singapore.
