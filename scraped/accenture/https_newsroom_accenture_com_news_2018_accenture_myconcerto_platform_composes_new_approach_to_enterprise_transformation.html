October 16, 2018

Accenture myConcerto Platform Composes a New Approach to Enterprise Transformation

NEW YORK; Oct. 16, 2018 – Accenture (NYSE: ACN) has introduced Accenture myConcerto, an insight-driven, integrated platform that helps organizations boost their ability to innovate, amplify business results and accelerate their journey to becoming intelligent enterprises.

Already in use with more than 60 clients across the globe using SAP solutions, Accenture myConcerto (formerly known as the Accenture Intelligent Enterprise Platform) brings together Accenture’s most disruptive ideas around industry, innovation and technology to deliver greater business value more quickly and easily.

Intelligent tools and pre-configured solutions for industry and business functions allow Accenture myConcerto to help harmonize disparate systems, processes, data and architecture against Accenture’s leading industry practices. It begins with design thinking to identify specific business challenges and differentiators. Automated diagnostics help to rapidly compose a personalized business case, roadmap and solution prototype.

Accenture myConcerto integrates seamlessly with Accenture myWizard to automate the provisioning of all project movements with tools, accelerators, preconfigured solutions and systems. During and after implementation, myConcerto continuously measures business value, provides new insights for continuous improvement and innovation, and enhances the client roadmap to take advantage of the latest innovations.

For SAP clients, the platform also includes SAP® Model Company services and uses SAP® Cloud Platform and SAP® Leonardo digital innovation system to accelerate time-to-value.

“To drive digital transformation and create exponential business outcomes, organizations need to be able to orchestrate their key business functions on a single, end-to-end intelligent platform,” said Bhaskar Ghosh, group chief executive, Accenture Technology Services. “Accenture myConcerto focuses on value realization. It brings together leading technologies, automation and industry experience from Accenture and our ecosystem partners to help clients reshape their businesses and reinvent themselves as intelligent enterprises.”

Accenture myConcerto will be showcased at the Accenture booth #16 at the International SAP
Conferences for Building Materials; Chemicals; Forest Products, Paper and Packaging; Mining and Metals, held October 16-18 in Prague, Czech Republic.
