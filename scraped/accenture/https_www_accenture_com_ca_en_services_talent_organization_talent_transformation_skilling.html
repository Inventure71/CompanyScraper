Why talent transformation matters

Emerging technologies, like generative AI, are transforming our world at an unprecedented pace and scale. Success today requires leaders to set and guide a vision for how to reinvent work, reshape the workforce and prepare workers for a generative AI world.

How talent transformation is changing

Rapid advancements in tech and AI are transforming the way work is done. Workers must adapt their skills if they want to stay competitive, especially when 44% of all US working hours are predicted to be impacted by gen AI. Never has there been a more urgent need for organizations to think ahead and provide people with the learning and skilling resources they need to seize these new opportunities.

What youll achieve

Faster execution

The right employee experience unlocks human ingenuity and attracts and retains talent. A great employee experience provides a deep sense of care, kindness and connection.
