Accenture labs recognized by fast company's world changing ideas

Accenture is honored to be recognized on Fast Companys World Changing Ideas 2024 list for self-drilling seed carriers for aerial seeding. The innovation relieves agricultural and environmental issues, improves soil quality and restores wildlands. Working alongside our partners at Carnegie Mellon University, Syracuse University, University of Pennsylvania and Zhejiang University, the team engineered a biodegradable seed carrier that enables aerial seeding of difficult-to-access areas.

Fast Companys annual World Changing Ideas awards honor businesses and organizations that develop creative solutions to the most pressing issues of our time. We are proud to be recognized by Fast Company for more than 10 World Changing Ideas. See how self-drilling seed carriers are driving global change.
