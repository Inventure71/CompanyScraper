October 09, 2020

Accenture Completes Acquisition of Myrtle Consulting Group


NEW YORK and HOUSTON; Oct. 9, 2020 – Accenture (NYSE: ACN) has completed its acquisition of Myrtle Consulting Group (“Myrtle”), an industrial operations consulting firm headquartered in Houston. Terms of the transaction, which Accenture announced on October 6, were not disclosed.

Myrtle expands Accenture’s manufacturing and supply chain capabilities. The company specializes in optimizing clients’ sourcing, production, supply chain operations and execution, and also has deep expertise in change management methodology, organization design and implementation, leadership development and frontline coaching. Myrtle’s team of more than 150 people joins Accenture’s Industry X practice.
