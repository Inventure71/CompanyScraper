Accenture global delivery network

We enhance client value through the use of advanced technology, including Generative AI and Intelligent Operations, backed by our unmatched industry and functional expertise.

Leading innovation across the world

With the largest and most diversified group of experts across Technology, Operations, Song, Strategy & Consulting, and Industry X, our unmatched delivery network is uniquely positioned to drive and scale innovation across the globe. Spanning over 50 delivery locations, featuring Advanced Technology Centers and Intelligent Operations Centers, we equip our clients with robust business resilience and strategic versatility across time zones to provide top-notch connectivity, language support and cultural understanding.

Our exceptional talent pool, combined with our deep industry knowledge and technology acumen, helps our clients navigate through each phase of their transformation journey. This approach not only accelerates our clients’ reinvention but also enables them to adapt to change effectively while unlocking significant business value.

How we create 360° value at speed and scale

Learn more about our formidable delivery capabilities.

Unrivaled industry and functional expertise

Our extensive knowledge spans 19 industries, enabling us to deliver tailored solutions that accelerate value creation. Our focused industry insight helps us understand the nuances of industry evolution, key business challenges, trends, operating models, processes and emerging technologies.

Global connected network

Our network includes Advanced Technology Centers and Intelligent Operations Centers across more than 50 locations worldwide, facilitating collaboration with clients in over 120 countries. This global delivery model ensures we provide competitively priced services and solutions across all business areas.

Future-ready top talent

Our exceptional talent is our greatest asset. As an innovation-driven company, we attract top professionals by offering expansive learning opportunities and a boundaryless career environment. This empowers our people to excel, continuously innovate and make impactful contributions.

Largest and most influential ecosystem

We partner with the world’s leading platforms, technology leaders, venture investments and academic institutions globally to enhance our services, expand our capabilities and deliver unique business value to our clients.

Intelligent delivery

Our approach integrates scalable innovation with standardized processes, automation, artificial intelligence and deep industry expertise with language fluency, proximity to clients and time zone advantages. This combination delivers cost-effective solutions while ensuring high quality, increased productivity, minimized risk, speed to market and predictability.

Pervasive innovation

Innovation is at the core of everything we do. We enable our clients to leverage cutting-edge technologies, capitalize on new opportunities, enter new markets swiftly and outperform competitors.
