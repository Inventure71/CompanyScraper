ADASTEC is a member of the Autoware Foundation

ADASTEC will support the Autoware Foundation by using/testing the new releases of Autoware during the development process and giving technical feedback. ADASTEC's contribution will enable users and developers of Autoware to improve their open-sources software platforms and projects' quality and functionality.

"Autoware is the leader in open-source autonomous driving software," said Dr. Ali Peker, CEO of ADASTEC. "We believe in the power of community. Joining the Autoware Foundation will enable us to work together with leaders of the automated driving community and share perspective and experience across different applications and components of automated driving."

ADASTEC will sit on Autoware Foundation's Technical Steering Committee, driving the strategic direction and technical priorities as a premium member.

"We are excited to welcome ADASTEC who is a technology leader for Level-4 automated transportation. Currently Autoware is designed for Level-4 autonomous vehicles. We look forward to collaborating with other members of the foundation and driving innovation in the field of autonomous transportation.
